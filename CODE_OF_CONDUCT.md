# Code of Conduct

### Our Commitment

This community is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for students, contributors, and maintainers.

We want this organization to be a safe place to learn, share knowledge, and collaborate on academic and open source work.

### Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- treat others with respect and professionalism
- communicate constructively and politely
- welcome contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels
- accept feedback in good faith
- focus on collaboration, learning, and community benefit

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- harassment, intimidation, or bullying
- discrimination based on background, identity, or personal characteristics
- abusive, insulting, or hostile language
- personal attacks or repeated disruptive behavior
- sharing private information without permission

### Scope

This code of conduct applies across this organization, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and other community spaces managed through these repositories.

### Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not align with this code of conduct.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the community.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it privately to the maintainers of the relevant repository or organization administrators.

All reports should be handled respectfully and reviewed in good faith.
